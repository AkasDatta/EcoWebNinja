The Ninja breaks digital barriers with WCAG 2.2 compliance!

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 help ensure websites are accessible for all users, including people with disabilities. The Ninja checks your website for contrast issues, missing ARIA attributes, keyboard accessibility, focus order problems, and incorrect heading structures – key elements required by the EU for equal access to digital content.
